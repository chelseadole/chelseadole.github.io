About

Data Engineering

I am a Data Engineer based in Seattle, currently traveling and working remotely. I discovered my passion for programing through research while pursing my B.A. in Economics and Politics at UC Berkeley. Following my graduation in 2017, I attended Code Fellows to further my skills, and have been working as an engineer ever since.


Presently, I am a Data Engineer at Coffee Meets Bagel: a dating app designed for people seeking relationships built on personal growth. At CMB, I architect, build, and deploy high-throughput data processing systems to support our user-matching ML algorithms. Currently, I am redesigning and rebuilding our feature engineering layer as an event-based system. My favorite things about my career are its focus on continuous learning, the ability to take responsibility and ownership for the work I perform, and the dedicated people with whom I work.
