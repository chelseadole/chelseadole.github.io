Lindy Hop and Jazz DJing

Lindy Hop

I started dancing lindy hop in September 2013, and haven't stopped since. I dance and teach as both a lead and a follow, and have social danced around North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia. My joy in lindy hop is derived from connecting with my partner, and interpreting the music together through conversation in dance.

Swing DJing

I started DJing swing-era jazz music while I was a student in the UC Berkeley college scene. In the years since, I have become a regular DJ at venues around the Bay Area and Seattle, including the 9:20 Special and the Century Ballroom. I have taught multiple DJ workshop series, DJ'ed for national and international workshops/exchanges, and acted as Head DJ. My favorite swing-era bandleaders and musicians include Ella Fitzgerald, Sidney Bechet, Count Basie, Artie Shaw, Hot Lips Page, and Earl Hines.
