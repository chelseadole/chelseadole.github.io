Lindy Hop/Jazz DJing

Lindy Hop

I started dancing lindy hop in September 2013, and haven't stopped since. Since then, I have choreographed for/danced in numerous groups (most notably the Sproul Stompers), danced around the U.S. and the world, and taught as both a lead and a follow. My joy in dance derives from connecting with my partner, and experiencing the music together in one irreplicable moment.
